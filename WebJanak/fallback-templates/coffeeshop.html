Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brew Haven began as a small neighborhood coffee shop with a big dream: to create a welcoming space where community and quality coffee come together.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms around the world, roasting them fresh daily to bring you the perfect cup every time.

Whether you're here for a quick morning pick-me-up or settling in for an afternoon of work, we're here to make your day a little brighter.
